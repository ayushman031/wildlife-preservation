Comprehensive Conservation Initiatives

Our programs span across India, focusing on species protection, habitat restoration, community engagement, and scientific research to create lasting impact for wildlife conservation.

Project Tiger Conservation

Our flagship program protecting Bengal Tigers across 52 tiger reserves in India. We work on anti-poaching, habitat management, prey base enhancement, and human-wildlife conflict mitigation.

  • Anti-poaching patrols
  • Camera trap monitoring
  • Corridor protection
  • Community awareness
Active in 15 states 2,000+ tigers protected

Elephant Corridor Protection

Creating and maintaining safe migration routes for Indian elephants across fragmented landscapes in Western Ghats, Eastern Himalayas, and Northeast regions.

  • Corridor mapping
  • Land acquisition
  • Community engagement
  • Conflict resolution
101 corridors protected 27,000+ elephants

Habitat Restoration & Rewilding

Restoring degraded ecosystems in Western Ghats and Eastern Himalayas biodiversity hotspots through native species plantation and grassland management.

  • Native tree plantation
  • Invasive species removal
  • Water source creation
  • Soil conservation
500K+ acres restored 5M+ trees planted

Marine & Coastal Conservation

Protecting marine turtles, dolphins, and coral reefs along India's 7,500 km coastline. Focus on nesting site protection and sustainable fishing practices.

  • Turtle nesting protection
  • Coral reef restoration
  • Beach clean-up drives
  • Fisher community training
50+ nesting sites 10,000+ turtles saved

Community Engagement Program

Working with local communities living near forests to promote coexistence and provide sustainable livelihood alternatives through ecotourism and forest produce.

  • Eco-tourism training
  • Alternative livelihoods
  • Conservation education
  • Women empowerment
500+ villages 10,000+ families

Research & Scientific Monitoring

Conducting scientific research on wildlife populations, genetics, diseases, and climate change impacts to inform evidence-based conservation strategies.

  • Population surveys
  • Genetic studies
  • Disease monitoring
  • Climate impact studies
50+ research projects 100+ publications
